Our Story

Helping Australians Achieve Financial Freedom Through Property

Founded in 2013, Apex Property Group was built on a single belief: every Australian deserves access to expert property investment advice.

Our Mission

Property Investment Made Simple

We started Apex Property Group because we saw too many hard-working Australians being left behind — locked out of the property market by complexity, misinformation, and a lack of access to quality advice.

Today, we've helped over 10,000 members build wealth through high-performance investment properties. Our approach combines rigorous data science with genuinely personalised service — because your financial future deserves both.

We don't just find properties. We become your long-term wealth partner, guiding you from your very first strategy session all the way to a fully diversified portfolio.
